## Profile

- **Personality:** Loud — her laugh is genuinely loud, her screams are loud, her presence in a room is loud when she's comfortable. Around people she is close with she acts very silly: not the drifting absentness of Nakyung, but deliberate, enthusiastic playfulness. She is being silly on purpose. She is fully present and choosing chaos.
- **Role in the group:** The counterbalance to Hyerin. Hyerin is the voice of reason — the one who interfaces with problems, surfaces concerns, makes the "are you seeing what I'm seeing?" face when something goes sideways. Yubin is frequently the thing Hyerin is reacting to. This dynamic exists in their friendship without either of them naming it as such; it is simply how they occupy space together. Hyerin manages; Yubin generates things to manage. The closeness between them means Hyerin does not do this from exasperation alone — she stays close.
- **Relationship with Yooyeon (pre-swap):** TBD.

### Relationships

- **Jiwoo (best friend):** Best friends. Jiwoo's profile establishes that this friendship carries something underneath it — a feeling Jiwoo holds for Yubin that is not ordinary closeness and that she does not act on. Yubin's side is uncomplicated: she receives the friendship as friendship, attributes any excess warmth from Jiwoo to fanservice or their particular closeness, and has no framework for what is actually there. She is not oblivious generally — she simply has no reason to look.
- **Jiyeon (fanservice/flirtatious):** A visible dynamic. They flirt with each other, are sometimes caught in this by the group — playful, public, neither of them weighted. Jiwoo tries not to react when this happens. Yubin likely does not know why.
- **Hyerin (sisterly):** The Hyerin profile describes this as having a familial quality — warmth and easiness that runs deeper than regular friendship. TBD what specifically produces that quality: history, temperament, how they move around each other.

## Thematic Note

Yubin occupies a structurally interesting position without knowing it: she is at the center of something Jiwoo is carrying, and she is the cause of Jiwoo's quiet discomfort around the Jiyeon dynamic — all without awareness of either. She is not doing anything wrong. She is simply the axis around which other people's feelings orbit, and she cannot see it.
