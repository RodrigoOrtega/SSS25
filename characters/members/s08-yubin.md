## Profile

### Performance Role

Strong performer, selected for Visionary Vision — the group's dance-focused unit — which reflects her capability. On stage she is documented as transforming noticeably: the self-contained, low-key person off stage becomes someone with a different quality of presence in performance. Members and observers have noted it explicitly. This is not effort she broadcasts; it simply happens when the context shifts.

She is not the group's strongest dancer overall — Kaede, for instance, sits above her by most accounts. But she holds her own in a dance-focused unit, which is the relevant measure.

### Personality

ISTP in practice: self-contained, precise, does not fill space she doesn't need to fill. Her voice has a natural low tone. She is funny in group content — she won Best Entertainer at the 2023 tripleS Awards, fan-voted — but the humor is dry and situational rather than performed. She does not set up bits. She responds to them, often with a single deflating line that lands because it is accurate.

The Queen Eyez naming moment is the clearest single snapshot of how she operates: Hyerin suggested "Big Eyez" as their pairing name; Yubin said her eyes aren't that big, so it became "Queen Eyez." She did not accommodate the flattery. The accurate version was better.

Physical tells: her ears turn red easily. She uses people she is comfortable with as body pillows — Jiwoo specifically is documented as one.

Hobbies: needle felting and acting. Both are solitary or absorbing pursuits, consistent with someone who recharges through focused, self-directed activity. She competed on a children's cooking show (I Am Chef) as a child and ranked in the top three, so cooking is a genuine long-standing skill, not an idol talking point. She dislikes carrots. Tteokbokki is her favorite food.

### Before tripleS

She was among the Source Music trainees in the team that would eventually debut as NewJeans. She has spoken about this period directly: she cried almost every day and her mental health was not good. She frames what came after as rebuilding — she says she cries less now and has regained her willpower. She was at JYP and HYBE as well before MODHAUS.

The existence of an alternate path she didn't take is part of her inner life, not something she carries visibly.

## Role in the Group

The counterbalance to Hyerin — Hyerin is the voice of reason, the one who makes the face when something goes sideways. Yubin is frequently what Hyerin is reacting to. Neither of them thinks of it this way; it is simply how they occupy space together. Hyerin stays close anyway, which says something about how the dynamic actually feels from inside it.

She is at the center of several things she does not know she is at the center of.

## Relationships

- **Jiwoo (best friend, "JjinChinz"):** They met in seventh grade while both studying acting — before either of them was in the industry. That friendship survived parallel trainee tracks at different companies and then being in the same group. When Yubin won Best Entertainer at the 2023 Awards, the prize was a staycation at a luxury hotel. She redeemed it with Jiwoo.

  The physical ease between them is documented: Yubin uses Jiwoo as a body pillow. This is catalogued as a personality fact, meaning it comes from content, not just reported. They were roommates in the mint room for a period; they have since moved to different hauses, which has not changed the dynamic.

  She is part of the Familyz trio — Yubin as the "mom," Jiwoo as the "dad," Joobin as their "daughter," named for a syllabic echo in Joobin's name and a physical resemblance that the group finds funny.

  Yubin has no framework for what Jiwoo is carrying. She receives the friendship as friendship. She is not oblivious generally — she simply has no reason to look.

- **Yooyeon (friends, "2Yooz"):** Among the original eight members, which means they have been in each other's daily orbit since the beginning. The closeness that built in that early period is real — shared space over time, before the group was fully what it is now. They are not each other's closest person, but the warmth is genuine and the ease is established.

  The Familyz trio produced a moment worth holding: Yubin accidentally called Yooyeon "mom" — the slip from the Jiwoo/"dad" dynamic bleeding into the wrong person. Yooyeon's response was immediate: *"So now I'm Joobin's grandma?"* The exchange is the best single evidence of what their dynamic actually sounds like — comfortable enough for that to land as a joke, not an awkward correction.

  They have shared a haus on multiple occasions without ever being roommates. The proximity is regular and low-stakes. Alex will encounter her this way: not dramatically, just constantly.

- **Hyerin (close, "Queen Eyez" / "BamBamz"):** They were actual roommates — the green room of HAUS 3. Of all her documented relationships, they share the most unit overlap: AAA, LOVElution, Visionary Vision. The matched nickname structure (Yubami / Hyebami) is their version of the dynamic in miniature — they found a parallel and settled into it without making it a thing.

  The sisterly quality documented in fandom comes from the roommate history, the age proximity, the dry mutual recognition they have developed. Hyerin is two years younger. It does not feel that way in how they move around each other.

- **Seoyeon (friends, "Bbobamz"):** Good friends. What specifically draws Yubin to Seoyeon is probably the contrast: Seoyeon has a playful energy that is different from the composure Yooyeon carries, different from Hyerin's reactive dryness, different from Jiwoo's expressiveness. With Seoyeon something is lighter and more spontaneous. They have shared a haus. Both in LOVElution.

- **Jiyeon (playful dynamic, "Kkaemulz"):** The pairing name means "biters" — Jiyeon named it because Yubin kept biting her. That is the documented origin. Physical, teasing, Jiyeon received it warmly enough to build a name around it. They are in the February Line together with Yooyeon (all three born in February), and in Visionary Vision together. They have not shared a haus.

- **Nakyoung (warm, "NyangNyangz"):** Won Best Couple together at the 2023 Awards, fan-voted. Through her time with Nakyoung, Yubin expanded her previously picky eating habits and is now considered one of the group's better eaters. That is a specific kind of influence — not dramatic, just real.

- **Mayu:** Nicknamed "Polar Chemistry" by the group — their MBTIs are opposites, ISTP and ENFJ. The label fits something true about how they operate around each other.

## Thematic Note

Yubin is at the center of several dynamics she cannot see. She is the person Jiwoo's unspoken feeling orbits. She generates the Jiyeon moments that Jiwoo watches from the outside. She is Hyerin's default Auguste without knowing she fills that role deliberately.

None of this makes her oblivious — it makes her someone who does not have a reason to look. She is self-contained, not self-absorbed. The things she cannot see are invisible because nobody has shown them to her.

For Alex, she is the low-stakes recurring presence who is most likely to catch something through accumulated contact rather than a single moment of recognition. She does not watch for emotional texture. But she is precise, and imprecision accumulates.
