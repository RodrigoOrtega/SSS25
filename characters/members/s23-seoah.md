## Story Notes

- The protected-space quality around SeoAh creates a specific situation for Alex. He is a 30-year-old man dropped into a body older than hers. His instinct to treat her carefully may be real and appropriate — but any version of it that is too visible, too deliberate, will read as strange coming from "Yooyeon," who has been in the group long enough that the protection instinct should feel natural rather than performed. He will have to calibrate, and he will not have the reference point to do it correctly.
- Her personality is currently under-defined. The "protected youngest" role describes how others relate to her — not who she is inside it. This should be developed before she appears in a real scene.
