## Profile

### Vocal Role

One of tripleS's two main vocalists alongside Dahyun. Before tripleS she trained at SM, JYP, YG, and FNC — more institutional exposure than most members came in with. The range is real: she has covered Dahyun's parts when needed. Not the most technically calibrated vocalist in the group by Dahyun's standard, but capable, and with a voice that carries emotional weight when she is in it.

### Personality

On the surface she is exactly what she says she is: a laughing machine. The face she makes when something catches her off-guard — eyes wide, mouth small, a beat of genuine surprise before the laugh arrives — has become a documented group fixture. She is very easy to make laugh. The threshold is low. Something vaguely funny, something she finds remotely funny, is enough.

But the trigger is more specific than it looks. She tends to laugh when she is embarrassed, or when something feels unfair to her. The laugh arrives in place of whatever the actual reaction would be — it comes first, and the feeling stays underneath. Her right eye twitches when the laugh is real. That is the tell.

She self-describes as funny and extroverted. There is truth to both without either being the full picture. The expressiveness is genuine — she does not perform reactions, she has them. But the INFP core means the internal world is richer and more private than the surface suggests. She processes deeply and quietly; the face she makes is real, but what is going on underneath it is not always the same thing.

She bites her tongue when concentrating.

### Before tripleS

Played competitive ice hockey on a junior team through late elementary and into middle school, before committing to idol training. She does not bring this up routinely. The group knows it exists as a fact about her; it surfaces occasionally and people find the gap funny. She does not need to explain that both things are simply true about her.

She and Yubin were both trainees at JYP at the same time before tripleS, and had been friends since middle school — they met through acting studies. That pre-group friendship is clean: just two people who knew each other, liked each other, and ended up in the same place.

### Baking

Good at it, specifically. Scones, cookies, meringue. In 2025 her parents bought her an oven and she made chocolate muffins for all 24 members on Valentine's Day. This is characteristically Jiwoo: expressing care through doing something rather than saying something. The members have eaten enough of her baking that the group has noted she could work as a barista if she had a different life.

### Other Details

- Dislikes alarm sounds; always wakes up first among EVOLution members when they are together; the one who turns off the lights when they leave for schedules

## Relationships

- **Yubin (best friend, feeling she does not act on):** Their friendship predates tripleS — best friends since middle school, acting studies, JYP trainees at the same time. That history is clean. Nothing unusual in it.

  The feeling developed later, when they became roommates in the mint room after Chaeyeon moved out. Shared private space, Yubin being fully herself without an audience, the daily accumulation of knowing someone that closely at the end of the day — that is when the friendship became something else on Jiwoo's side.

  She knows what it is. The clarity is complete; she is not suppressing it out of uncertainty. She has also thought through the practical reality: acting on it carries risk that is not only personal. A revealed relationship between two members enters the group's social ecosystem and the fan economy simultaneously. Group dynamics change. Some fans disinvest. Those consequences do not belong only to the two of them. She has done this math and arrived not at anguish but at acceptance: the situation is what it is. They spend most of their time together as it is. The friendship is warm and real. She finds what exists between them sufficient, and she is genuinely okay with it — not resigned, but settled.

  Whether they ever have a direct conversation about it, or whether the feeling eventually becomes something acted on, is open. There is no urgency on her end. She is not waiting, not building toward anything. She is simply here, with what she has.

  Yubin has no framework for what Jiwoo is carrying. She receives the friendship as friendship and attributes everything else to their particular closeness or to the register they have with each other. She is not oblivious generally — she simply has no reason to look.

- **Chaeyeon (good friends, former roommates):** They shared the mint room before Yubin moved in. Jiwoo's description is "tika-taka on point" — constant natural back-and-forth, same energy level. She pranks Chaeyeon most frequently of anyone. Their bedtime routine when they were roommates was joking around until the end and then saying "Sweet Dreams~" before sleep. It is an easy, playful friendship that did not require the proximity to survive. What Jiwoo has with Chaeyeon is the version of closeness that does not complicate anything — warm, reliable, light.

## Thematic Note

Jiwoo's situation is an inversion of Seoyeon's: Seoyeon confessed and received an unanswered question in return. Jiwoo has not said anything and may not. Both are carrying something for a person they are close to — but through completely different postures. Seoyeon's is grief wearing management. Jiwoo's is clarity wearing acceptance. Neither has resolved what they're holding.

The practical constraint Jiwoo operates under is worth separating from the emotional one. The reason she does not act is not purely personal. The idol context adds a collective dimension that she takes seriously. This is not excessive caution — it is an accurate reading of how the economics work, and she has made a considered choice. She is not a person in denial. She is a person who has done the math and found the current situation livable, even good.

**Middle A.** Her INFP depth means the betrayal of trust runs real — the EVOLution intimacy was not nothing. But she also has a temperament that has already settled one unresolved thing (the Yubin feeling) by deciding the situation is what it is and finding it livable.
