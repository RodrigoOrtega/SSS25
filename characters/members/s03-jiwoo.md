### Relationships

- **Yubin (best friend, unreciprocated feeling):** Jiwoo and Yubin are best friends. The relationship has moments that step past the boundary of that — gestures, closeness, something in how Jiwoo behaves around her that exceeds what friendship typically produces. Jiwoo is aware of this. She notices where the line is and that she crosses it. Yubin is not aware — she attributes everything to fanservice or ordinary closeness, and has no framework for the feeling Jiwoo actually carries. The dynamic is not dramatized within the group; it lives underneath, and Jiwoo keeps it there. Whether she wants to keep it there indefinitely, or whether the weight of it is building, is TBD and worth establishing before writing Arc 3.
- **Chaeyeon (good friends):** A genuine friendship, warm and comfortable. TBD how it differs in texture from the Yubin relationship — what it gives Jiwoo that the other doesn't.

## Thematic Note

Jiwoo's situation is an inversion of Seoyeon's: Seoyeon confessed and got no clean answer. Jiwoo has not said anything and may not. Both are carrying something unresolved, but through completely different choices. The contrast is worth holding when writing both in the same space.
