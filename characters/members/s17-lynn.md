## Profile

- **MBTI:** ISFP — Quiet, generous, present-moment; gives without tracking it; loyal to what she values. The kindness is not overhead — she values the people around her being okay, and she acts on it.

- **Appearance:** 172cm — one of the tallest members, roughly third in the group behind Xinyu and Jiwoo. Her representative emoji is the shark (🦈). That choice is accurate: her face and bearing are sharp and composed, with clean lines and a precise quality that reads as cool or controlled at first encounter. Her actual personality is the gap. Members have described her as "wacky, kind and cute" — the wacky is the thing that surprises people who only see the exterior. She doesn't look like someone who drops all composure around food, but she does. Fans have called her the "hidden mukbang queen" for exactly this reason: she eats enthusiastically and unselfconsciously, and the contrast with how she looks doing it is part of the charm. She dislikes spicy food and scary things.

- **Personality:** The member most often described as the kindest. This is not a reputation she maintains or performs — it is simply what she does. She gives her share of food to someone who wants it. She shows up for people without being asked and without tracking it afterward. She does things for herself too; she is not a martyr and she is not self-erasing. She just genuinely wants the people around her to be okay, and acts on that want. The giving is effortless in the sense that she does not experience it as a cost. Underneath the kindness: something a little wacky, something particular in how she sees the world. She is quieter than most in group settings but not self-effacing — she has her own internal logic and she follows it.

- **Communication:** Prefers phone calls over texts. For someone quiet, this is meaningful — she would rather be present with someone in real time than manage an asynchronous exchange. She is not someone who maintains a careful distance through a screen.

- **Music:** Her personal taste runs to indie Japanese rock — Mrs. GREEN APPLE, Creepy Nuts, back number, Official Hige Dandism, GReeeeN. These are not idol acts. They are artists recognized in Japan for lyrical and musical craft. She has genuine musical references that sit outside the genre she is working in.

- **Anime:** A documented fan. Her character affinities include Maomao from *The Apothecary Diaries* (quiet, pragmatic, observant — operates by her own internal logic), Howl from *Howl's Moving Castle*, and Gelatoni (a quiet, imaginative Duffy & Friends character). The pattern across these is consistent: she is drawn to self-contained characters who follow their own rules without announcing it. She has referenced anime in content and it is a genuine interest, not incidental.

- **Fanservice:** Does not initiate it, does not shy away from it. When Mayu turns it on her — which is often — she goes along without hesitation and without it meaning anything for either of them. This is the group's most consistent running dynamic; it has become comfortable through repetition. She occupies a middle space among the members: not a generator of fanservice, not someone who declines it. She participates comfortably when it comes to her and lets it go just as easily when it doesn't.

- **Dance:** Pre-selected by MODHAUS for Visionary Vision — one of only two members the company chose before fan voting opened. This is a recognition of her specific dance skill. She was identified as a cornerstone of that unit's identity. The selection was not through public vote; the company chose her.

- **Background:** Trained briefly at SM Entertainment before passing MODHAUS's online audition in Japan and choosing to come to Korea in March 2023. She also trained at YA Dance Studio. The decision to choose MODHAUS was motivated by the company's enthusiasm toward her. She has a younger sister born in 2008.

### Relationships

- **Mayu (S16 — close, MayuLynn):** The flirtatious dynamic between them is the group's most visible version of their relationship, but it is not the heart of it. The actual closeness is quieter than the fanservice. Mayu initiates; Lynn participates comfortably and without it mattering. The pairing is named, fan-recognized, and has been consistent enough to become part of the group's fabric. They share J-Line (Japanese members in tripleS). The 14cm height difference is part of what makes the dynamic funny when it plays out.

- **Nakyoung (S7 — 77z, significant):** Named for the shared digit in their S-numbers (S7, S17). Before they became close, Lynn had named Nakyoung as her bias within tripleS — she admired her from a distance before they were peers. They are both in Visionary Vision. The friendship has an emotional texture that comes from that arc: there is a before and an after, and the after is the kind of closeness that forms between people who share both a daily living situation and a creative project. Lynn knows what it felt like to admire someone and then find herself alongside them.

- **Kaede (S9 — J-Line):** Both Japanese members. Both introverted within the Foreign Line grouping, which is otherwise more extroverted. The shared introversion within a louder cluster creates a quiet bond.

- **Shion (S20) and Sullin (S22) — 06z:** All three are born in 2006, making them the youngest birth year cohort in the group — younger than Jiyeon (S24, born 2004) despite lower S-numbers. The S-number reflects reveal order, not age. Within 06z: Shion is oldest, then Lynn, then Sullin.

- **Jiyeon (S24 — Moon unit, friends):** Both in Moon orbital unit — proximity built the friendship. Jiyeon is two years older than Lynn by birth year. The closeness is real; its specific texture is TBD.

- **Yooyeon (S5 — thin, Visionary Vision):** Both pre-selected or selected for Visionary Vision. At Day 0, their relationship is warm but not deep — they have not had a sustained shared context that would have built something specific yet. Yooyeon is the group's informal anchor; Lynn's giving quality means there is no friction between them. But the relationship is thin in practice. Visionary Vision is where this changes: sustained rehearsal, shared creative pressure, and daily proximity in a smaller group will build something that has not yet had the chance to form. Alex inherits a relationship with Lynn that is essentially at the beginning — which means he is not failing to reproduce an established dynamic so much as failing to build one that Yooyeon would have built. The trajectory Alex closes off is more load-bearing than what he inherits.

## Story Notes

- **The gap in the exterior.** When Alex first encounters Lynn, the shark face will read as one kind of person. What she actually is will reach him differently — probably through something small, the food enthusiasm or a reference he doesn't expect. He has no wall designed for someone who gives without asking. His practiced exits from intimacy are built for people who make demands or need management. She doesn't.

- **Visionary Vision as the context where things develop.** The rehearsal schedule, the smaller group dynamic, the sustained proximity — this is where a Lynn–Yooyeon relationship would have formed and where an Alex–Lynn dynamic will form instead. He is not performing a relationship that already exists; he is encountering someone who would have come to know Yooyeon through exactly this process. The difference between what she gets and what she would have gotten is something she will feel before she can name it.

- **The Nakyoung thread.** Lynn knows what it feels like to admire someone from a distance and then become close. She would bring that specific attentiveness to Nakyoung into any shared VV context. Alex does not know this about either of them.

## Faction (Post-Reveal)

**Middle B.** Nothing in her profile generates disgust-based enmity. Her instinct is to give, to not track it, to remain present. The Muhaez orientation (shared with Shion) — a stated wish not to hurt anyone — means she cannot easily adopt a hostile position. She starts middle and drifts toward genuine warmth for Alex over time, probably among the fastest-moving Middle B members toward practical ally behavior, even if she doesn't formally commit. The VV closeness that develops with Alex may be the specific context that moves her.
