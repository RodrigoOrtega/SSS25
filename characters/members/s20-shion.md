## Profile

- **MBTI:** ESFJ — Harmony-oriented, attuned to the group's emotional state, stable and consistent; holds her role in the social fabric without having to assert it; cares about people through presence and continuity rather than gesture. The soft-spoken quality is not introversion — it is Fe pointed inward toward the group's equilibrium. She knows what she likes, she knows it thoroughly, and she has been this way long enough that everyone around her has simply incorporated it.

- **Voice:** Her speaking voice is soft, childlike, and calm — genuinely, not performed. She has spoken about people making fun of it and has come to recognize that as bullying, not a legitimate criticism. The understanding is settled: she knows they were wrong. She has not changed her voice and does not intend to. What the experience left is less a wound than a clarity — she is not self-conscious about it, but she has been made aware, more than most, of the specific experience of being reduced to a surface quality. That awareness lives quietly alongside everything else.

  The voice is also active. She has voice imitation as a documented specialty — she can reproduce vocal tones and registers despite the soft baseline. The baby voice is her natural speaking register; what she can do with her voice is a different matter. ASMR content featuring her voice has been shared officially, and the softness that reads as unremarkable in a noisy group context becomes something else when the room is quiet.

- **Personality:** Deliberately gentle. This is the more accurate version of "soft-spoken." She is not passive or uncertain — she held leadership of the NXT sub-unit, she carries content on her own, she exercises every morning with consistency. The softness is a value she holds, not a limit she works around. The Muhaez pairing with Lynn is the clearest evidence: both self-described themselves as people who tend to take care of others, and renamed the pairing from "06Motherz" to Muhaez — from the Korean for "harmless" — because both share a specific wish not to hurt anyone. That is not a personality trait; it is a stated orientation. She moves through the group with that in mind.

- **The food identity:** Her other consistent nickname is Epicurean. The bread interest already in her file is a subset of a much larger food engagement: tteokbokki, burgers, cream bread, cake, scones, gukbap, jjimdak, fried chicken, pizza, meat, cookies, desserts, ice cream. She ran a Food World Cup in a SIGNAL live (cake won). She dislikes food waste and finishes what others leave. The morning exercise is by her own account to support the eating. Food is not a bit — it is a real and consistent engagement that she is organized around.

- **Creative background:** From elementary school through high school, she was a member of the broadcasting club. She graduated from Hanlim Multi Art School in February 2025 in film making and video production. Her specialties are filming and editing video, voice imitation, interior decorating, and restaurant finding. She photographs others and environments — not herself. The creative identity is behind-the-scenes: she sees the room and records it rather than putting herself in the frame.

- **Anime:** Yumeiro Patissiere (a girl training to become a pastry chef — the food-art overlap is not incidental), Shugo Chara!, Crayon Shin-chan (specifically Whitney, an innocent and childlike archetype), Pororo (Petty). The pattern across these is soft, imaginative, food-adjacent or childhood-adjacent. She is not drawn to edge or darkness in what she watches for pleasure.

- **Singing:** One of the best vocalists in the group, sitting just below Dahyun and Kaede in overall ability. The voice that reads as quiet in conversation carries clarity and control in a recording or live performance.

- **Background:** P NATION trainee before joining MODHAUS. Passed the audition for *My Teenage Girl* (Mnet survival show) but withdrew before filming. Attended GB Academy from May 2020 to September 2023.

## Story Notes

- **The voice and the awareness it produced.** She knows what it feels like to have a surface quality used against her. That specific experience — being reduced to how you sound rather than what you are — gives her a particular attentiveness to moments when someone is being defined too simply. She may not be the first to name what is wrong with Alex's version of Yooyeon, but she is likely to notice the dissonance between presentation and person before she has words for it.

- **The Muhaez orientation in a post-reveal world.** Her stated wish is not to hurt anyone. The reveal puts her in a situation where every available position involves hurting someone — Alex, or Yooyeon, or Seoyeon, or the group's stability. A person whose core orientation is harmlessness will not find a comfortable place in a three-faction conflict. She will hold the middle not out of indifference but out of an inability to choose a harm. This is different from undecided — it is stuck.

- **The bread/food thread.** Already noted in the prior file: a low-stakes scene gift. A conversation between Alex and Shion about bread, where he happens to know something she doesn't expect Yooyeon to know, is the kind of moment that builds something before either of them is ready for it.

- **The behind-the-scenes eye.** She films people, photographs environments, notices what is in the room. She is not doing this with suspicion; it is just how she processes. The footage she could have in her head of "Yooyeon before" and "Yooyeon after" is not something she will necessarily act on — but it is there.

## Faction (Post-Reveal)

**Likely undecided middle, anchored by the Muhaez orientation.** She will not be able to choose a side that requires hurting someone. The enemy faction requires directed anger she does not carry. The ally position requires comfort with the situation she may not yet have. She will hold the middle not strategically but structurally — because every exit from it costs something she is not willing to spend.
