## Profile

Kaede is loud in all the ways that matter — physically, expressively, in the space she takes up on stage. She arrived in Korea as a teenager, the first foreign member of tripleS, and she built herself into the group through energy rather than ease. The language was a wall she knocked down by being impossible to ignore. By Day 0 she speaks Korean without friction, though she'll sometimes pause a beat before landing on a word — not a hesitation, just her brain switching tracks. It doesn't slow her down.

Her personality is a particular combination: she hates losing and wants to be loved by everyone. Those two things coexist in her without apparent contradiction. The competitiveness is real — she goes after things, in performance especially, with a focus that members notice. The approval-seeking is equally real, and it isn't a performance of warmth; it's the genuine underlying wish. She was the first foreign member, which meant she had something to prove before she had a foundation to stand on. She built both simultaneously. The wish to be loved by everyone is what that experience left in her — not insecurity exactly, but a specific attentiveness to whether she's received.

She is physically affectionate in a casual, unguarded way — will walk up to younger members and touch their cheek or pat their head before wandering off, the way you do with someone when your guard is so far down the gesture doesn't register as a gesture. She chased members around a fansigning with a toy hammer once. This is also Kaede.

- **MBTI:** INFP — idealistic, values-driven, emotionally sensitive underneath the high-tension exterior. The expressiveness isn't contradiction; it's how her idealism externalizes. She wants a world where people smile and feel good, and she tries to produce that wherever she is.
- **Dance:** One of the group's best — arguably the best, by some members' accounting. When someone asked the group who the top dancer was, heads turned to her before the answer was spoken. Her style is hip-hop with real swag; she makes large, clean movements, deliberately pushing her physicality outward to compensate for her height (161cm). A dance crew watching her noted they could "feel her soul in her posture" — she brings emotional truth to movement rather than just executing choreography. She will notice a body lying about itself. She watches how people move the way some people watch faces.
- **Performance role:** Main dancer in LOVElution. In Visionary Vision — a dance-focused unit — she carries both main dancer and main vocalist responsibilities alone. The ad-libs are hers. For a unit selected specifically for dance, she's its spine.

### Relationships

- **Dahyun (closest):** They lived together in the Blue Room. Proximity became something real — Kaede has said she "feels most confident being with Dahyun." For someone who came from Japan at seventeen into a group of Korean peers, that kind of confidence is not a small thing. They confide in each other; the exchange runs both ways. Members describe their chemistry as "healing." The bond isn't the early-safety bond that fades once you've found your footing — it held and deepened. Kaede has said she was "in love with Dahyun's singing," which is the kind of thing that reveals how much you've been paying attention.
- **Chaeyeon:** Expressed wanting to get closer because "they're both hyper." A self-aware observation — she recognizes her own energy and gravitates toward its mirror. Whether that friendship deepened is TBD.
- **J-Line (Kotone, Mayu, Lynn):** The Japanese members have their own thread of solidarity within the group. Kaede is the youngest in that subset by some distance. It's not a clique — they're fully integrated — but there's a specific ease between them that comes from shared origin.
- **Yooyeon (pre-swap):** Warm, cohort-level closeness. Kaede was part of the original S1–S10 group that went to Yooyeon's hometown together — that kind of trip builds group trust rather than a particular bilateral bond. Yooyeon is one of the people who made the group feel like home early on, the way she does for most members. Kaede trusts her, likes her, is comfortable with her. She is not in the innermost circle the way Dahyun is. Post-swap, this means Kaede will be a slower reader of the wrongness — but she will read it, because she reads bodies.

## Faction (Post-Reveal)

**Middle A.** Her INFP idealism does not produce disgust. Her wish to be loved by everyone runs structurally against holding contempt — an enemy position would require her to close off someone she is in the same room with daily. She can't easily sustain that. But she processes through emotion first and may need time before she can look at Alex without complicated feeling. She starts middle and moves gradually.

Dahyun (her closest person) is in the ally haus. The separation is the real cost for Kaede — not the faction dynamics themselves but the daily distance from the person who makes her feel most confident.
