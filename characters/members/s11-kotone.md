## Thematic Note

Kotone is one of the few members who has thought explicitly about what the performance layer produces in audiences, and decided not to participate in one specific form of it. Her reasoning is external and practical — she understands fan culture and doesn't want to feed certain dynamics. This makes her different from members who simply don't think about it, and different from members who choose not to engage for personal reasons. She has a clear-eyed view of the mechanism and has opted out deliberately. For Alex, who is performing constantly and involuntarily, this is worth holding: Kotone interacts with people in a register that is not mediated by that layer. She is not performing at you, and she is not inviting you to perform back. That could land as relief or as exposure depending on the moment.
