## Profile

- **MBTI:** ENFP-T — Warmly enthusiastic; sees connections and possibilities; talkative; earnest to the point of saying true things before she has calculated the cost. The turbulent variant is accurate: she is not all confidence underneath the warmth. She cried repeatedly thinking through the fromm situation before she wrote what she wrote. The feeling comes out fully before the strategy has a chance to form.
- **Voice:** She has a naturally deep, low speaking tone — an incongruity against the extroverted, expressive ENFP register. She and Sohyun have a named pairing, *Low Notez (저음즈)*, specifically because of their shared deep voices. This is not an affectation or a borrowed behavior; it is simply how she sounds, which surprises people who hear her before they know her. It means her warmth arrives in a register that is calmer than the energy beneath it.
- **The mimic:** The most specific and consistent thing about Kotone as a person is that she reproduces people. Not as a prepared performance — it happens spontaneously, in domestic situations. During a casual meal at MOD Forest HAUS she imitated Hayeon's laugh on the spot, to Hayeon's delighted surprise. In a SIGNAL episode she imitated multiple members accurately enough that the group and fans gave her the nickname "Mimic Genius." She does this without announcing it: she absorbs how someone sounds, how they hold a specific moment, and at some point it just comes out. The group has come to expect it the way you come to expect any reliable behavior in someone you know well. For writing purposes: she reads people deeply. She cannot imitate someone she has not been paying close attention to. The mimicry is the evidence of the attention.
- **Earnestness:** She does not communicate carefully. Her Instagram output in July 2024 was 30 posts in 31 days. She created a separate Twitter account so fans could download higher-resolution photos. After her GP999 elimination, she entered a public KakaoTalk open chat to thank fans, did not realize how it worked, exited, re-entered, and exited again — the social media unfamiliarity of someone doing something genuine without the architecture for it. The fromm messages in November 2025 (after Day 0 in story terms, but characterizing) are the clearest version: she wrote directly to fans that she came to Korea specifically to perform on music shows, that music shows were the reason she wanted to debut in Korea at all, that she had cried repeatedly before writing it. MODHAUS placed her on hiatus immediately after. She said it anyway. She did not calculate what it would cost first, or if she did, she said it anyway.
- **Warmth without theatricality:** Shion (S20) is Kotone's bias within tripleS — specifically because of the positive energy her smile gives off. Chaeyeon watched Stranger Things, which she found scary, because Kotone liked it. These are investments made toward her without Kotone having done anything spectacular to earn them. The magnetism is ambient and consistent rather than produced. She does not need to perform arrival when she walks into a room.
- **Kissing evasion:** Among the members who are comfortable with physical fanservice — specifically Mayu and Hayeon, who will produce lip-adjacent contact with minimal provocation — Kotone playfully tries to escape. Not as a principled refusal, not with any weight placed on it. It is closer to a cat avoiding being picked up: she sees it coming, she moves. The group has incorporated this as a behavioral fact about her. It is not a rule she explains, just something she does and everyone knows.
- **Crying:** Kotone cries easily when overwhelmed by the intensity of a feeling — happiness, sadness, either. The trigger is not the valence of the emotion but the weight of it. When the feeling is big enough, it comes out. She is not embarrassed by this and does not try to suppress it. The group teases her out of it when the context calls for it — recorded content, public settings — not cruelly, just enough to redirect. She takes it fine. This is consistent with her Arc 4 scene: when Alex finishes explaining and she cries softly, it is not grief specifically — it is the weight of what she just heard reaching the threshold. In that room, nobody will tease her out of it.
- **Role in the group:** She anchors four named pairings simultaneously: NootNootz (Jiyeon), Low Notez (Sohyun), 999z (with Nien and Xinyu from Girls Planet 999), and 04z (with Jiyeon and Chaeyeon — the three 2004-liners). That is not a person who manages relationships selectively. She accumulates them the way she accumulates observations: without effort, without losing track.

### Background

Kotone grew up in Kokubunji, Tokyo. Her family name, Kamimoto, has Okinawan roots. She has a Korean registered name: Park Tone (박토네). She taught herself Korean from junior high school through Korean TV, dramas, and YouTube — not formal study. "Didn't enjoy formal studying; learned while playing." She then spent years failing Korean agency auditions before Girls Planet 999 gave her an opening. She competed as an individual trainee — no agency backing, not the standard path — and was eliminated in the semi-finals at rank 24, but was saved at one point by the Planet Pass (judges intervening specifically to keep her in). Korea and K-pop was a deliberate, sustained, years-long pursuit. She was already watching tripleS content and was a fan of the group before becoming S11. She worked part-time at a restaurant before her idol career.

## Thematic Note

The mimic quality is the foundation for her Arc 4 role, not the fanservice evasion. She cannot imitate someone she has not been paying close attention to — the mimicry is the evidence of a sustained, absorbed attention to how people actually are. She has been paying that attention to Yooyeon for the duration of their friendship: how she sounds, how she holds a silence, the specific texture of her responses. When Alex is performing Yooyeon, Kotone is looking at someone she has that depth of read on, and what she sees does not match. Not in a single moment, not through a single slip — through the accumulated wrongness of someone she knows well being slightly off in every direction at once. The mimic genius knows the difference between a person and an impression of that person. Alex's impression is not bad enough to fail immediately and not good enough to pass indefinitely. Kotone is the one who has the most specific reference data against which to check it.

For Alex, Kotone's presence will feel different from the members who interact through the group's warmer performance register. She is not not-warm — she is genuinely warm, visibly so. But the warmth is attentive rather than expressive. She watches while she talks. He will feel observed in a way he cannot account for, without being able to name the mechanism.

## Role in the Reveal (Arc 4)

**Kotone is the first member to discover the truth.**

Her discovery comes not through emotional attunement but through seeing clearly. The mimic quality means she has a precise, absorbed read on how Yooyeon actually is — how she sounds, how she holds a silence, the specific texture of her responses. Alex is performing Yooyeon constantly. Kotone looks for the actual Yooyeon and doesn't find her. The gap between what is being performed and what she expects to find is not explained by any ordinary change.

- **Discovery trigger:** TBD — must be something that closes off every other explanation. Not behavioral accumulation alone; something that makes replacement the unavoidable conclusion.
- **Confrontation:** She approaches Alex directly, shaken but not explosive. She does not immediately accuse — she asks. *"What do you mean you're not Yooyeon?"* arrives before Alex has confirmed anything. She has already concluded.
- **Response to the truth:** She holds silence until Alex finishes explaining. Then she mourns — cries softly, not dramatically. Grief and understanding coexist in the same sitting; she does not need to separate them. She arrives at the conclusion that Alex is a victim too.
- **Decision:** Agrees to help where possible. Agrees to keep the secret initially. Recommends telling Seoyeon — an innocent suggestion, not strategic. She does not know the romantic history between them.
- **What she doesn't know:** The recommendation she makes is catastrophic in its consequences. She gave it from genuine care. She will have to live with that once she sees what it produces.
- **Next step:** Tells Mayu. The two of them form the core of the ally structure.
- **At the group meeting:** Kotone does not lead the structural response — Mayu does. Kotone adds her voice in support, standing with Mayu when she steps forward. Her guilt about the chain she set in motion (discovery → told Mayu → recommended Seoyeon → this room) is real and present in the meeting. She does not use it as the reason she speaks; it surfaces differently, in how she relates to Alex in private afterward.
- **The guilt and whether it belongs to her:** The recommendation to tell Seoyeon was innocent — made without knowledge of the romantic history, from genuine care for Seoyeon. Whether Kotone actually did something wrong is not straightforward. She carries the guilt anyway, because she is someone for whom the weight of a feeling arrives before the logic of it. At some point she will have to reckon with whether the guilt belongs to her or whether she has been holding something that was never hers to carry. That reckoning is its own arc.
- **Faction:** Allies.
