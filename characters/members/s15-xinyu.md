## Profile

- **MBTI:** ENTP — Quick, conceptually-oriented, outspoken; finds novel solutions; driven by ideas and engagement. Her curiosity is not self-conscious — she pokes into what people are doing because she's genuinely interested, not because she's performing interest.
- **Role in the group:** TBD — expand as arc develops.
- **Relationship with Yooyeon (pre-swap):** Friendly but not among the closest. Yooyeon probably sits in Xinyu's top twelve closest members but not top six. The warmth is real, but it's ordinary group warmth rather than a specific intimacy they've built. Alex has more room to maneuver with Xinyu than with members who knew Yooyeon closely — she would need sustained specific exposure to land on something being wrong, not just different.

### Physical Appearance

- **Height:** The tallest member of tripleS.
- **Face and build:** Reads as conventionally model-like — feminine-featured, well-proportioned. The combination of height and that quality gives her a specific kind of presence. Sohyun occasionally measures herself against this without Xinyu knowing.

### General Presence

- **Social energy:** Golden retriever quality — she moves through rooms with easy, unself-conscious curiosity, checking in on what people are doing, plugging into what's already happening rather than commanding the space. She doesn't need to be the center of the room; she just shows up in it, interested in everything.
- **Physical affection:** Uses her height to hug members from above — wrapping around them rather than meeting them at eye level. The gesture is instinctive, not calculated. She does this broadly, not selectively. It means her specific physical warmth toward Sohyun doesn't read as obviously different from how she is with everyone else.
- **What others think of her:** Easy to be around. The curiosity doesn't feel invasive; the warmth doesn't feel performed. The group is comfortable with her. She does not create friction.

### Emotional Register

- **Crying:** Xinyu is a sympathetic crier — she cries when others cry, when people share something heavy with her, when she's moved by someone else's feeling. The mechanism is emotional porousness, not personal distress. She doesn't resist it or feel embarrassed. The group teases her out of it when they're recording or in public settings — not cruelly, just enough to snap her back, because the crying is real and can run if nobody redirects it. She takes the teasing fine.

### Relationships

- **Sohyun (best friends, real feelings):** Best friends. They perform as a married couple in fanservice contexts — Xinyu's side of this is not performance. She genuinely likes Sohyun. The public expressions of affection, the married couple framing, the way she acts toward her — all of it is real feeling channeled through a form that has plausible deniability. She has not told Sohyun how she actually feels. Her reasoning is simple: she does not want to scare Sohyun away if Sohyun doesn't feel the same. The friendship is too important to risk. So the fanservice becomes the channel — the only form in which she can express what she feels without requiring an answer, because it is officially just performance. She is not aware that this arrangement also means she will never find out. She is not thinking about the ceiling; she is thinking about not losing what she has. Her general warmth and physical affection with everyone else gives her additional cover — her Sohyun-directed affection doesn't stand out as different from the outside.
- **Nien (friends, language):** They bonded through shared language — both speak Mandarin, and were introduced to the group in close succession (S15, S13). The friendship has the specific comfort of being able to speak naturally, without managing register or translation. TBD what else has grown around that foundation.

## Thematic Note

Xinyu's situation is structurally parallel to Jiwoo's — a real feeling, undeclared, managed to protect the friendship — but through the opposite method. Jiwoo suppresses and withdraws; she gives less of herself to keep things safe. Xinyu expresses through performance; she gives more of herself, but in a form that can always be dismissed as not-real. Both are protecting the friendship. Both are also, by different routes, ensuring the feeling stays unanswered. Xinyu's version is arguably more generous — she gives Sohyun something real even if Sohyun can't receive it as such — but it is also the one that can go on indefinitely without a resolution point.
