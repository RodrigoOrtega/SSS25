## Profile

- **MBTI:** INFP — Idealistic, values-driven; loyal to people who matter to her; inward attention that wanders freely; does not push on others' inner lives because she respects the privacy of her own. Fits precisely: the drifting attention is Ne wandering; the not-pressing-Yooyeon quality is Fi knowing better than to intrude.
- **Personality:** The group's butt of affectionate teasing — not for stupidity but for a particular quality of clumsy, forgetful, sometimes absent presence. She misses cues, loses things, arrives slightly behind where she was supposed to be. The specific texture of it is that her mind drifts to inconsequential things: whatever she ate a few days ago, a specific memory that surfaced for no reason, the shape of something she passed on the way in. She is fully attending — just to something that doesn't matter. It is not a named condition, not a pattern she can interrupt; it is simply what her attention does when it wanders. The teasing is warm, not mean; it is the kind that exists because the group is comfortable with her. She receives it well — doesn't perform annoyance, doesn't bristle. She is settled about herself in a way that makes her easy to be around.
- **Fanservice:** She is not someone who initiates flirtatious dynamics. It is not part of how she participates in group performance or group affection. When other members initiate fanservice with her, she is receptive — not awkward, not resistant — but she lets them lead. She is a willing object of the performance without being its driver.

### Relationships

- **Yooyeon (friends):** A friendship built from complementary parts. Yooyeon's intelligence and composure produce a particular kind of stability; Nakyoung's goofy, absent quality makes Yooyeon laugh — genuinely, not politely. Nakyoung is also one of the few people Yooyeon can exist next to without performing: she can rest her head on Nakyoung's shoulder while thinking hard about something, and Nakyoung will not ask what she's thinking about. She doesn't push. She is simply there. Nakyoung is not consciously aware that she occupies this role for Yooyeon — she is drawn to Yooyeon for the security she brings and behaves accordingly, without either of them naming what the dynamic is. She finds Yooyeon beautiful; she compliments her looks openly and is sometimes seen staring. More often than not the staring is just Nakyoung lost in her own world, but it lands the same way either way. When Yooyeon does something Nakyoung doesn't like — takes a position she disagrees with, makes a choice that bothers her — she gets cutely sulky about it. She doesn't argue or press; she just goes slightly quiet and soft in a way that is visible. Yooyeon generally notices.
