## Profile

- **MBTI:** ENFP — Warmly enthusiastic and imaginative; makes connections faster than she can stop herself voicing them; processes the world by externalizing it. The associative, continuous talking is Ne running aloud — the connections arrive before she decides whether to voice them, and she voices them.

- **Personality:** The group's acknowledged "best yapper" — a label that made it into official content, meaning the group owns it collectively and she owns it about herself. The texture of the talking is specific: she is most talkative when telling stories involving herself, and she continues even when no one has responded to the previous thing she said. One thread leads to another without a destination — she will describe a cookie, rank every cookie she has ever had, recall the circumstances around each, follow a memory that surfaces, and continue from there for several minutes without a point and without needing one. She is not performing; she is just running. Soomin is also chatty, but the register is different: Soomin fills a room with energy and warmth. Yeonji narrates. She processes the world by externalizing it as it arrives.

  She has a reliably bright disposition. Members who have been around her consistently say they rarely see her looking depleted or low. Yooyeon specifically described her as "the most spirited one among the maknae line and someone who is mature for her age" — a characterization that sits in interesting tension with the Tobot love and the Crayon Shin-chan preference, and points to something in her emotional attunement rather than in her manner or tone.

  The talkativeness is social, not constant. She reportedly prefers to do things quietly when alone. The continuous narration runs when there are people to run it toward; without an audience it goes elsewhere.

- **Fanservice:** Does not participate in the physical fanservice common among some members. Appears to have adopted this in proximity to Kotone, who has a principled reason for opting out. Whether Kotone explained that reason to Yeonji, or whether Yeonji simply observed and followed the behavior without the reasoning behind it, is not yet established. She does not make a point of it — she just doesn't engage in that register.

- **Background:** Scouted through social media by MODHAUS around October 2022; auditioned and was accepted as a trainee the following morning. She had no prior dance or singing training before this. Everything she developed as a performer was built within the MODHAUS system in a relatively short window. She is not someone who spent years preparing to be an idol, which means she arrived without the normalized behaviors and expectations that prolonged training tends to install.

- **Piano:** Has played since age 7. Was the pianist in her school band club. Musical Theatre major at Hanlim Multi Art School (transferred April 2024). The piano identity is her longest-running skill — older than her idol career by years.

- **Interests:** Piano, food (kimbap, tteokbokki, egg rolls, ice cream, malatang, meat), mint chocolate, Tobot (Korean animated robot franchise), Crayon Shin-chan. The song that first introduced her to K-pop was BTS's "Answer: Love Myself."

## Thematic Note

Yeonji's verbal style — associative, continuous, without destination — is the opposite of Alex's internal register. He processes slowly and in private; she externalizes everything as it arrives. In shared scenes, that gap will be felt. She is not asking him to match her; she just talks. But someone performing constantly and monitoring every response will find her volume difficult: she produces too much, too fast, and expects nothing particular back — which should be a relief but won't feel like one.

The initial-cold-impression she had of Yooyeon is a story asset. She calibrated to Yooyeon over time and concluded she had been wrong about the distance. She may apply that same generous misreading to Alex for longer than most — buying him time he didn't earn.
