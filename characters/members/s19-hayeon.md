## Profile

- **Personality:** The member who most fully embodies fanservice — she generates it naturally, she's good at it, and she has a clear trajectory: she has kissed, or been close to kissing, most of the group. This is not reckless. She has thought about the fact that some people find this uncomfortable, and before initiating with any member, she asks privately whether they're okay with it. The asking happens off-camera, in a quiet moment — not as a formal negotiation, just a direct and considerate check. Members who aren't comfortable don't get pushed; members who are comfortable get Hayeon in full effect. The "mission" framing is how she talks about it lightly, a self-aware joke about her own tendency. The consent-checking is not a joke. It is what makes the whole thing not a problem.
- **Fanservice record:** Has kissed (on lips): Mayu, Dahyun, Xinyu, Soomin, Nakyung, Chaeyeon. Has had non-lip moments with: Chaewon, Joobin, Hyerin. The list of members not yet on either side is still open.
- **With Yooyeon:** Conditional clearance. Yooyeon would allow it in a specific staged context — a couple skit, a fanmeeting cosplay moment, something where the situation sets it up. Not casually, not on a whim. Hayeon knows this. She does not push outside that frame.
- **Friendship with Chaewon:** Very close — the friendship has a quality that is almost romantic. Neither of them names this. Whether the not-naming is deliberate management or just how it has always been is ambiguous, and the ambiguity may be load-bearing: naming it might change something neither of them wants changed. TBD whether this ever tips.
- **Role in the group:** Generates energy. She is not managing the group or stabilizing it — she is producing moments, making things happen, keeping the room alive. Zenith orbital unit connects her daily to Jiwoo, Yubin, Soomin, Yeonji, and Joobin.
- **Relationship with Yooyeon (pre-swap):** Not among her casual fanservice targets; the dynamic is specific and context-dependent. TBD how close they are outside of that frame.

## Story Notes

- **The Alex problem:** Hayeon has conditional clearance with Yooyeon. This means a situation that fits the frame — a couple skit at a fanmeeting, a cosplay moment — is exactly the kind of scheduled event Alex will eventually have to attend. He will not be able to preemptively stop it. He will have to respond in character. This is one of the sharper edges in Arc 3: not the casual flirting he can deflect, but a staged, expected moment that "Yooyeon" has already agreed to. He will have no preparation and no clean exit.
- **The consent-checking quality as Alex's unexpected safety net in other cases:** For most members, Hayeon would never push without clearing it first. If she has not explicitly cleared something with "Yooyeon" beyond the conditional frame, she will not go further without asking again. This means Alex actually has a small protection he did not set up and does not know he has — at least outside the contexts Yooyeon had already agreed to.
