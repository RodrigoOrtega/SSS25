## Profile

- **MBTI:** ENFP — Outgoing, enthusiastic, genuinely curious about people; makes connections between them and draws them together; warmth that is not just immediate but reaching — she is interested in who people are, not just in how the room feels right now. Her connector quality is ENFP: she doesn't just fit into dynamics, she generates them.
- **Personality:** Bright, cheery, openly warm. Her energy in group settings is high and easy — she doesn't perform this, but it isn't effortless either. She calls herself "Vitamin N" — extreme E, brings energy to the group, a mood maker by member consensus. That description is accurate as far as it goes. What it doesn't capture: the brightness runs continuously and it costs something she doesn't make visible.

  The cost has a specific origin. She arrived at tripleS through a path that had a rejection in front of it — Girls Planet 999, the survival show where she competed and didn't make the final lineup, before eventually finding her place in the group through a different door. She debuted in LOVElution as the newest among members who already had experience. She named this in her debut press conference — the nerves, the feeling of arriving behind. The warmth is genuine and it is also functional: she learned early that running toward belonging warmly and consistently is how you close that gap. At some point the earning became the way she moves through the world. She no longer thinks of it in those terms. But the habit is structural, not incidental.

  What this produces: someone who accommodates across a wide range of registers and people without friction, not because she is shapeless but because she is genuinely at ease and genuinely practiced at being at ease. She has a pre-performance breathing ritual that doesn't make it into the public presentation. She said of the US tour that it was "tiring but definitely worth it" — the acknowledgment of cost is real, and brief, and followed immediately by the forward-facing thing.

- **Role in the group:** Connector. She navigates the group broadly and without difficulty. She fits into different unit dynamics without requiring adjustment from the people around her. Members describe her as the group's mood maker and a vitamin for morale. This is function as much as it is trait.

- **Interests:** Watches girl group MVs as a hobby — genuine fandom, not industry research. Her formative reference is Girls' Generation; SNSD is what brought her to K-pop before she imagined performing in it. She engages with the emotional and narrative content of music: she described listening to "Speed Love" while imagining scenarios of girls experiencing romance. She watches dramas. She is also a documented fast runner — won gold at ISAC 2025 in the women's 60m dash (silver the year before). The athletic identity is not incidental; it is specifically hers in a way that has nothing to do with performance.

### Relationships

- **Xinyu (S15 — closest friendship, predates tripleS):** They met on Girls Planet 999, before either was a tripleS member. The friendship was built during the show and continued after; Xinyu wrote Nien a letter in 2024 reflecting on three years of friendship since they first met. The fan pairing (XiNien) is one of the most recognized in the group. The Mandarin they share adds a specific ease — Nien named it explicitly, saying she relied on it a lot — but the foundation is longer and more emotionally substantive than language alone. They were both in LOVElution and are both in Visionary Vision. This is Nien's most important relationship in the group.

- **Seoyeon (S1 — close, Sunnyz pairing, 03z):** They have a specific established dynamic in fan-facing content — a named "shy couple" bit, fanservice they perform as scene partners rather than distributed warmth. The Sunnyz pairing is fan-recognized and fan-awarded. The closeness is real within those terms; what exists underneath the performance layer is something quieter and less examined. What is structural: they share LOVElution, neptune orbital unit, the 03z friendship with Dahyun, and the ComeTruez tour group. Seoyeon is one of Nien's closest Korean-native members, and that closeness has been accumulated across a lot of shared contexts. The safety of the fanservice dynamic — warmth with no real demand — functions for both of them, though for Seoyeon it carries additional weight she probably hasn't examined.

- **Dahyun (S10 — 03z):** The three of them — Seoyeon, Dahyun, Nien — are all 2003-born, all LOVElution members, all neptune orbital unit members. They named themselves "Re-O-Re" (reverse Oreo) in a neptune content live, a self-assigned group name from a specific moment. They traveled together on the LOVElution US tour and the ComeTruez tour. The Nien-Dahyun axis within the trio isn't documented with its own specific texture beyond the group context; it is a friendship built and sustained in shared settings.

- **Kotone (S11 — 999z, GPP999 alumni):** Kotone, Nien, and Xinyu are all former Girls Planet 999 participants now in tripleS — the 999z trio. Nien and Kotone were not particularly close during GPP999 (different groups, limited overlap); the friendship developed after both were revealed as tripleS members and began living together. They are both in Visionary Vision.

- **Yooyeon (S5 — shared understanding, Visionary Vision):** They are together in Visionary Vision. What characterizes their relationship specifically: both arrived at tripleS through a path that had a prior rejection in front of it — Yooyeon from an earlier contest/audition process, Nien from Girls Planet 999. That shared experience is not something they needed to discuss at length; it is the kind of thing communicated in how you respond to certain situations. The ease that came from it — the specific quality of being recognized by someone who knows the same particular thing without having to explain it — is real. It is also the thing Alex will not be able to reproduce. He will know Nien is in his unit. He will not know what Yooyeon knew, or why it mattered.

## Thematic Note

Nien's fanservice dynamic with Seoyeon is relevant background for Seoyeon's emotional state at Day 0. The performed intimacy is safe precisely because it makes no real demand — it is warmth without stakes. For Seoyeon, who is carrying an unresolved and unanswered thing with Yooyeon, that safety has a specific function even if it is not examined consciously.

The Yooyeon relationship is load-bearing for Arc 3. The ease Alex fails to reproduce won't be visible to him as a failure — he won't know what he's missing. Nien will feel the absence without being able to name it, at least initially.

## Faction (Post-Reveal)

**Middle B.** Her personality is the deciding factor: the connector quality is structural — dividing the room runs against how she moves through the world. Her ENFP instinct does not produce sustained contempt. She ends up in Middle B, not the enemy haus. The loyalty pull toward Xinyu and Seoyeon is real and is felt, but it does not override her personality under personality-first analysis.

The cost of this: she is in a different haus from both her closest friends. She is the member of Middle B most likely to spend time in the enemy haus as a visitor — not as a defector, just as someone who cannot hold the separation cleanly.
