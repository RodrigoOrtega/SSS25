## Profile

- **MBTI:** ENTP — Quick, outspoken, generates energy and situations; notices her own patterns even when that awareness doesn't produce behavioral change; dislikes routine. The chattiness, the mischief, and the self-aware checking-in all fit.
- **Personality:** Chatty and warm, with a tendency to fill any silence she's in. Flirts lightly with members — not with intent, more as a default register of affection; it is part of how she shows she likes someone. She has a playful streak: light pranks, low-stakes mischief, the kind of humor that lands because it is affectionate rather than sharp. She is not subtle. She doesn't especially try to be. Her chattiness resembles Yeonji's on the surface — both fill the room with words — but the engine is different. Yeonji's output comes from internal association processing outward; Soomin is simply energized by people. Put Yeonji in a quiet room alone and she is fine. Put Soomin there and she is restless.
- **Self-awareness:** She knows she can be a lot. She checks in with members about it — a genuine, recurring habit, not performative. She wants to know she isn't crossing a line. She rarely actually tones it down when reassured; the checking-in functions more as permission than as recalibration. The members have been fond of her long enough that they receive it as one of her antics — the checking-in itself included.
- **Daily circle:** She spends most of her time with Hyerin, Chaewon, Hayeon, and Yeonji. These are her default companions — the people she moves through the day with in the loose, unscheduled time between obligations.
- **Role in the group:** TBD — expand as arc develops.
- **Relationship with Yooyeon (pre-swap):** She admires Yooyeon. There is something specific in how she relates to her — not just friendly closeness, but genuine regard, a looking-up quality. Yooyeon is among the very few members who receives Soomin's full energy with complete patience; most people hit a saturation point she doesn't. That patience has registered with Soomin, even if she has never named it aloud. Yooyeon is older — the same-age ease Soomin has with Hyerin doesn't apply here. The admiration has a directional quality: Soomin toward Yooyeon, not mutual in the same register.

### Relationships

- **Yooyeon and Seoyeon ("daughter" dynamic):** In the group's fanservice vocabulary, Soomin is positioned as the "daughter" of the Yooyeon-Seoyeon pair — a playful fiction built on the visible dynamic between all three. She participates in it willingly and with genuine affection. What she doesn't know is the history underneath the pair she's been assigned to: the confession, the fight, the calcified drift. The performance she plays into has a real shape she has never seen. Alex will inherit Yooyeon's half of this dynamic without knowing what Seoyeon brings to it.
- **Yeonji (close friends):** TBD — relationship established, texture to be filled in.
- **Hyerin (close friends, same age, early history):** The same age — 동갑 — which in Korean social terms means equal footing from the start, no hierarchy, the informal register that makes a different kind of closeness possible. They have been together since near the beginning of tripleS: S2 and S6, both in the room during the formative period before the group was fully what it is now. The friendship is foundational in that sense — not built on a specific incident but on the accumulated weight of shared early time at the same level. Within the friendship, Soomin teases Hyerin the way she teases other members: light pranks, prodding for reactions. What makes Hyerin a particularly satisfying target is the return — the visible judgment face, the disbelief, the amplified reactions Hyerin produces. Soomin doesn't think of herself as playing a specific role; she's just doing what she does, and Hyerin happens to be a good audience. She doesn't know she is Hyerin's default Auguste, or that the dynamic she generates is something Hyerin leans into deliberately. She simply knows the reactions are worth it. By now the dynamic is so settled that neither of them would think to describe it — it is just how they are together.

## Thematic Note

Soomin will be one of the early stress-tests for Alex in Arc 3: she is in the habit of being received a certain way, and she monitors whether that reception changes. If he handles her differently — shorter, more distant, less fluent in the warmth Yooyeon extended — she will notice. Not as suspicion at first. As a small wrongness she'll try to compensate for by being more of herself.
