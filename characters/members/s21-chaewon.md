## Profile

- **MBTI:** INTP — Theoretical, internal, comfortable holding contradictions without resolving them. She holds the gap between her aesthetic and everything else about her without needing to explain it. That's INTP comfort with internal consistency over external legibility.
- **Personality:** The embodiment of the "am I cute?" persona — pink, strawberries, very girly, fully inhabited. She is not performing femininity ironically and she is not at war with it. It is just who she is, and she is completely comfortable in it. She knows she is cute. She invites you to agree.
- **The alien thing:** Members, fans, and Chaewon herself have all independently landed on the same word for her: alien. Not 4D, not quirky — alien, as in operating on logic that is internally coherent and externally baffling. Jiwoo (S3) has said she is the most otherworldly of all 24 members — someone who catches people off guard regardless of time and place. This is not at odds with the cute persona. The cute persona is the surface; the alien is what's underneath it doing its own thing. She is shy with strangers, then becomes one of the loudest people in the room once comfortable — Hyerin has called Chaewon, Hayeon, and Soomin "the noisy ones" within their 07z group, despite Chaewon being the INTP and theoretically the reserved one. The shift is not a contradiction she is aware of or bothered by. She just is different things at different temperatures.
- **Over-immersion:** When something has her genuine interest, she does not modulate for the social register she's in. During a co-live stream with Hayeon, Hayeon casually equated sea otters and river otters. Chaewon responded with an extended, detailed explanation of exactly how and why they are different, and why she specifically likes sea otters and not river otters. Unprompted. In full. This is not exceptional behavior for her — it's just how her attention works when it lands on something she cares about.
- **Sleep:** Listed as a special skill on her official Cosmo profile: sleeping 24 hours a day. Self-reported and consistent with who she is. Hobbies are watching movies and reading — solitary, internally recharging activities. She values stillness. The cute energy and the ability to vanish into a bed for a day are the same person.
- **The nunchucks:** Her father is Kim Sung-chan, a decorated Teukgong Moosool (TGMS) practitioner and director of a TGMS gym. She trained under him. She holds 3rd-degree black belts in both TGMS (a Korean special forces martial art combining taekwondo, hapkido, and judo) and hapkido, with trained nunchaku use. This is not a random hobby — it is a family inheritance, built across years. She does not lead with it in conversation; it surfaces when context creates an opening. When it does surface, she occupies the authority role without apparent self-consciousness. In October 2024, a tripleS YouTube episode put her in the instructor position — teaching Soomin, Yubin, and Hayeon self-defense and sparring. The format was comedic by design, but she did not play either register against the other. The cute and the capable sat side by side without her needing to manage the mismatch for anyone. At a Haidilao meal following her Hanlim graduation ceremony, she spontaneously began spinning handmade noodles in a flashy martial-arts style. The members present burst out laughing. She likely did not think of it as a performance.
- **Strawberries and pink:** Specific, real preferences, not a character bit. Strawberries are a comfort. Pink is not a phase she grew out of. She is consistent.
- **Friendship with Hayeon:** Very close — the friendship sits at the edge of romantic without either of them naming it. This is not tension being suppressed; it is something both of them inhabit comfortably without needing to define it. The not-naming is not avoidance — it may simply be that the category doesn't fit, or that fitting it into a category would change something neither of them wants changed. What they have works. In Badge War Season 3, they were in competition against each other at one point; Chaewon still rooted for Hayeon when she reached the final. TBD whether one of them carries more awareness of the edge than the other.
- **Fanservice:** Hayeon's record notes a "not on lips" moment with Chaewon — meaning close but the specific line hasn't been crossed, perhaps because their actual closeness makes that version of it feel different. TBD how Chaewon experiences and navigates that distinction.
- **07z:** The four tripleS members born in 2007 — Hyerin (April), Chaewon (May), Hayeon (August), Soomin (October) — are a named friendship group. All attend or attended Hanlim Multi Art School except Hayeon (Lila Art High School). Hyerin is the teacher/leader figure within the group. The other three, including Chaewon, are the ones Hyerin describes as noisy.
- **Glow unit dynamics:** In Glow (with Sullin S22, SeoAh S23, Jiyeon S24), the most documented specific dynamic is with Sullin. Sullin arrived with very limited Korean; Chaewon is monolingual. A circulated clip shows them attempting to communicate for roughly two minutes — gestures, expressions, effort — with the rest of Glow present. Fan-named "SullWon." The framing is affectionate. No other specific inter-member dynamic within Glow is well-documented at this stage.
- **Relationship with Yooyeon (pre-swap):** No specific bilateral moments documented. They share the Sun orbital unit (with Mayu, Hyerin, Chaeyeon, Xinyu), which is their primary shared context. There is a six-year age gap; Yooyeon is from the 2001 line and was part of tripleS's founding generation. Chaewon entered the group in 2024. In Sun, Chaewon is the maknae surrounded by members several years older — a different generational register than her two other main contexts (07z, Glow), where she is among peers. How she calibrates within that older-member dynamic is not yet established.
