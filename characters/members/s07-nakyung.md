## Profile

- **Personality:** The group's butt of affectionate teasing — not for stupidity but for a particular quality of clumsy, forgetful, sometimes absent presence. She misses cues, loses things, arrives slightly behind where she was supposed to be. The specific texture of it is that her mind drifts to inconsequential things: whatever she ate a few days ago, a specific memory that surfaced for no reason, the shape of something she passed on the way in. She is fully attending — just to something that doesn't matter. It is not a named condition, not a pattern she can interrupt; it is simply what her attention does when it wanders. The teasing is warm, not mean; it is the kind that exists because the group is comfortable with her. She receives it well — doesn't perform annoyance, doesn't bristle. She is settled about herself in a way that makes her easy to be around.
- **Fanservice:** She is not someone who initiates flirtatious dynamics. It is not part of how she participates in group performance or group affection. When other members initiate fanservice with her, she is receptive — not awkward, not resistant — but she lets them lead. She is a willing object of the performance without being its driver.
